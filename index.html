About US

Lord Ganesha, the beloved elephant-headed deity, is revered as the remover of obstacles and the god of wisdom, prosperity, and new beginnings. Known as Vighnaharta (the destroyer of obstacles) and Buddhipriya(lover of wisdom), Ganesha's blessings are invoked before starting any auspicious work.This sacred space is dedicated to spreading the divine teachings, miracles, and timeless wisdom of Ganapati Bappa.
